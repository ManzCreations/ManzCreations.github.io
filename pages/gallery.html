Washington Summer Festival

Set against the beautiful backdrop of Little Washington's waterfront, we were honored to be part of this cherished community celebration. This vibrant summer festival brought together local artisans, vendors, and the community for two days of waterfront festivities. Our booth showcased designs inspired by coastal living and the charming spirit of this historic waterfront town.

Mayberry Days® Festival - Mount Airy, NC

We were delighted to be part of this beloved festival celebrating the spirit of Mayberry in Andy Griffith's hometown. At this family-friendly event, we brought our crafts to a celebration that captures the charm and simplicity of a bygone era. Our booth featured nostalgic designs and custom pieces that perfectly complemented the festival's warm, small-town atmosphere.

We joined the vibrant community of Bunn, NC at their Spring Fling event held at the Bunn High School Football Stadium.
