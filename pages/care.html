Product Care Guide

Learn how to properly care for your Crafted 4 You products to ensure their longevity and maintain their beauty.

Freshies & Car Fresheners

Essential Care Instructions

  • Immediate Use: Hang freshies immediately after receiving or store in a cool, dry place.
  • Temperature Sensitivity: Do not place on direct surfaces like a car dashboard in direct sunlight due to potential melting from extreme temperatures.
  • Safety: Keep away from children and pets - contents are not consumable.

⚠️ Important Warnings

  • Do not use in wax warmers
  • White freshies may discolor over time with certain scents
  • Avoid trapping between sun shade and windshield

Cups & Tumblers

Care Instructions

  • Washing: Hand wash only with mild soap and warm water
  • Avoid: Do not put in dishwasher - this can damage the design and insulation
  • Cleaning Tips: Use soft cloths or sponges - avoid abrasive materials
  • Drying: Dry thoroughly immediately after washing

Maintenance Tips

  • Regular cleaning prevents buildup and maintains appearance
  • Avoid soaking the tumbler for extended periods of time
  • Keep away from extreme temperatures
  • Store upright when not in use

Photo Slates

Care Instructions

  • Cleaning: Dust with a soft, dry microfiber cloth
  • Display: Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Handling: Hold by edges to avoid fingerprints on the image
  • Storage: Store upright in a cool, dry place on the provided stand

Additional Care

  • Avoid harsh cleaning products
  • Do not expose to moisture
  • Handle with clean, dry hands

Glass Cutting Boards

Care Instructions

  • Cleaning: Hand wash with warm, soapy water after each use
  • Drying: Dry immediately to prevent water spots
  • Storage: Store in a vertical position when possible
  • Usage: Use on a stable, flat surface

Important Notes

  • Avoid dropping or impact to prevent cracking
  • Do not use abrasive cleaners
  • Safe for food contact

Wind Spinners

Care & Maintenance

  • Installation: Secure firmly in an appropriate location. Avoid any obstancles that might prevent the metal to spin.
  • Weather: Consider bringing indoors during severe weather. Direct sunlight might cause color fading.

Regular Maintenance

  • Check hardware connections monthly
  • Inspect for signs of wear or damage
  • Clean debris from spinning mechanisms

Car Cupholder Coasters

Care Instructions

  • Cleaning: Hand wash gently with mild soap and water when needed
  • Drying: Air dry completely before placing back in your vehicle's cupholder
  • Daily Care: Remove any spills promptly by blotting with a clean cloth
  • Storage: Store in your vehicle's cupholder or in a clean, dry place when not in use

Care Tips

  • The thick cloth material is designed to be durable and long-lasting
  • Spot clean when possible to maintain the coaster's shape and design
  • Avoid wringing or twisting the coaster when wet
  • Allow to dry naturally - do not use heat or dryer

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Do not machine wash or dry
  • Do not use bleach or harsh cleaning products
  • Avoid excessive soaking which might affect the coaster's structure

License Plates

Care Instructions

  • Cleaning: Use mild soap and water
  • Installation: Use proper mounting hardware
  • Maintenance: Regular cleaning to prevent buildup
