Product Care Guide

Learn how to properly care for your Crafted 4 You products to ensure their longevity and maintain their beauty.

Freshies & Car Fresheners

Essential Care Instructions

  • Immediate Use: Hang freshies immediately after receiving or store in a cool, dry place.
  • Temperature Sensitivity: Keep away from extreme heat and direct sunlight to prevent melting.
  • Positioning: During hot days, move freshie to gear shift or store in an open bag.
  • Safety: Keep away from children and pets - contents are not consumable.

⚠️ Important Warnings

  • Oils may cause staining on fabrics and surfaces
  • Do not use in wax warmers
  • White freshies may discolor over time with certain scents
  • Avoid trapping between sun shade and windshield

Cups & Tumblers

Care Instructions

  • Washing: Hand wash only with mild soap and warm water
  • Avoid: Do not put in dishwasher - this can damage the design and insulation
  • Cleaning Tips: Use soft cloths or sponges - avoid abrasive materials
  • Drying: Dry thoroughly immediately after washing

Maintenance Tips

  • Regular cleaning prevents buildup and maintains appearance
  • Avoid soaking the tumbler
  • Keep away from extreme temperatures
  • Store upright when not in use

Photo Slates

Care Instructions

  • Cleaning: Dust with a soft, dry microfiber cloth
  • Display: Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Handling: Hold by edges to avoid fingerprints on the image
  • Storage: Store upright in a cool, dry place

Additional Care

  • Avoid harsh cleaning products
  • Do not expose to moisture
  • Handle with clean, dry hands
  • Use proper hanging hardware for wall display

Glass Cutting Boards

Care Instructions

  • Cleaning: Wash with warm, soapy water after each use
  • Drying: Dry immediately to prevent water spots
  • Storage: Store in a vertical position when possible
  • Usage: Use on a stable, flat surface

Important Notes

  • Avoid dropping or impact to prevent cracking
  • Do not use abrasive cleaners
  • Safe for food contact
  • Dishwasher safe, but hand washing recommended

Wind Spinners

Care & Maintenance

  • Installation: Secure firmly in an appropriate location
  • Cleaning: Regular cleaning with mild soap and water
  • Protection: Apply UV protectant coating annually
  • Weather: Consider bringing indoors during severe weather

Regular Maintenance

  • Check hardware connections monthly
  • Lubricate moving parts as needed
  • Inspect for signs of wear or damage
  • Clean debris from spinning mechanisms

Beaded Car Charms

Care Instructions

  • Handling: Handle gently to prevent bead separation
  • Cleaning: Wipe with a soft, dry cloth
  • Storage: Store in a cool, dry place when not in use
  • Protection: Avoid prolonged sun exposure

Additional Tips

  • Check attachments regularly
  • Keep away from direct heat
  • Avoid pulling or stretching
  • Remove during car washing
